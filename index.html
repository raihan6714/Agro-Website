About Wins Agro Limited

Aries Agro Ltd. was founded by Dr. T. B. Mirchandani and Mrs. Bala Mirchandani in 1969. We are the business of manufacturing of micronutrients and other customized nutritional products for plants and animals. We started our activity by manufacturing a small range of mineral feed additives for animals & birds and then diversified into mineral additives for the agriculture use.
